A little about myself!

Hello and Welcome to my cosmic portfolio! My name is Talha 'Moon' Zia and this is where I share my cosmic journey with you guys by displaying few of my best astrophotos taken over the yeras. My astrophotos have been featured on Spaceweather.com, NASA's APOD Astronomy Picture of the Day, forbes website, space.com, photographingspace.com, Earth&Sky.org, thenews.pk, dawn.com, in Heelal and Astronomy magazines, and in a book.
